## Create topics command:
kafka-topics.sh --create --topic orders.crude_oil --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --partitions 3 --replication-factor 1
kafka-topics.sh --create --topic trades.crude_oil --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --partitions 3 --replication-factor 1
